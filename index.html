About us

What

Zaroor, our trademarked retail solutions, aims to transform existing Kirana shops into Just In Time inventory enabled modern convenience stores. This will truly bring modern retail in nooks and corners of India

How

Using technology, analytics and automation, s-mart will convert general trades in to modern trades. Technology will help in creating customer delight via personalized offers, hassle-free ordering. Analytics will help in JIT inventory. Automation will help in managing complex operations.

Why

General trade kirana stores are fighting proverbial David vs Goliath battle against well funded, large, modern trade companies. We, at s-mart, believe although local shops have great sales skills they need help on shop operations. We enable them to fight and win this battle.
